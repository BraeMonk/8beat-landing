Get Started in Minutes

Follow these steps the first time you open 8-Beat Studio. Once you're comfortable, unlock Pro to access everything permanently.

1. Launch & Pick a Vibe

  1. Launch the studio using the button above
  2. Choose a genre preset (Lofi, Hip-Hop, Ambient)
  3. Navigate between tabs: Generate, Sequencer, Mixer, Effects, Harmony, Multitrack, Samples
  4. In the Generate tab, click to create your starting groove

2. Shape Beat & Harmony

  1. Open Sequencer and toggle steps to taste
  2. Use Alt/Shift/Ctrl for velocity & probability
  3. Switch to Harmony tab and set key, scale, and octave
  4. Build progressions by tapping chords or using templates

3. Add Samples & Arrange

  1. Open Samples and choose or randomize a kit
  2. Edit parameters: pitch, decay, tone, noise, distortion
  3. Load samples into sequencer ‚Äì they follow your harmony
  4. Use Multitrack to arrange and record audio

4. Mix, Polish & Export

  1. Balance channels in the Mixer tab
  2. Add reverb, delay, and filters in Effects
  3. Export full mix or multitrack stems
  4. Download high-quality audio files
